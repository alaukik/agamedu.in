FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

What operations can Agam track for my institute? ▾
If you are a training centre or coaching institute, you can expect most operations to be tracked: enquiry & follow-up, admissions, fee management, batch operations, lecture scheduling, attendance, exam schedules, and exam results.
How does pricing work — is it per user? ▾
Pricing is per centre/branch — not per user. You can have unlimited users at one centre. Charges only increase if you add more branches.
Are there any hidden costs? ▾
No. The only costs are the plan fee and any optional add-ons you choose. No setup fee, no hidden charges.
Is there a discount for annual payment? ▾
Yes — pay annually in advance and you pay for 11 months but get 12 months of access. That's one full month free.
What implementation support do you provide? ▾
We provide full implementation support — multiple online sessions to understand your operations, configure the system, migrate your existing data, and train your team. Support is included during and after the free trial.
What are the accepted modes of payment? ▾
We accept NEFT/RTGS bank transfer and cheque. We do not accept cash payments.
How long is the free trial? ▾
15 days of full access, all features unlocked, with implementation support included. No credit card required to start.
